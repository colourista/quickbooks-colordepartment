Color Department QuickBooks Integration

A private internal integration for Color Department LLC accounting review and reporting workflows.

Privacy Policy Terms Connect Disconnect

Purpose

This integration connects Color Department LLC to QuickBooks Online through Intuit OAuth so authorized company operators can review company accounting metadata, reports, invoices, expenses, and related records for internal bookkeeping, audit, and planning workflows.

Access model

The integration is intended for internal use by Color Department LLC. Access is granted only through Intuit's official OAuth authorization flow. The integration does not ask users for QuickBooks passwords, MFA codes, or browser session cookies.

Default safety policy

The integration is operated in a read/audit-first mode. Any write, create, update, or delete action requires explicit human confirmation before execution.
